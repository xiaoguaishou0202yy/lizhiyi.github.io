GIS Analyst & Map Maker

Originally from Suzhou, China, I have had passion for creating maps and conducting GIS analysis, particularly in the areas of natural disasters and urban health. I hold a Master’s degree in Cartography and Geographic Information Systems from the University of Wisconsin – Madison, where I developed expertise in spatial data analysis, geovisualization, and GIS programming. My research experience includes studying the impacts of natural disasters, as well as exploring urban health issues such as air quality and community safety.
